exp.xml is used for leveling

 

structure:

 

<level>

   <lv>1</lv>

   <c>0</c>

   <j>0</j>

   <w>0</w>

   <statusPoint>0</statusPoint>

</level>

 

lv:Level

c:Required CEXP

j:Required JOB EXP

w:Required Weapon EXP

statusPoint: Additional Stat points for current level, not used in current version of Saga
